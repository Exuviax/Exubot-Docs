What is Auto Moderation?

Auto-Moderation helps you keep your discord free of common spam; by allowing moderators to activate Auto-Moderation in any channel of their choosing and specify individual channel settings without affecting any other channels on the server. Usage Examples

Sick of trying to learn and just want to copy and paste? Here are the Auto Moderation Usage Examples

Auto Moderation Modes
Action Off Light Active Moderate Insane
Link Off On On On On
Capital Off Off On On On
Emote Off Off Off On On

Usage !mod mode <mode name>
Link Protection Usage
Action Command
ON !mod protection links on
OFF !mod protection links off
WHITELIST ADD !mod protection links whitelist add <url>
WHITELIST REMOVE !mod protection links whitelist remove <url>
WHITELIST VIEW !mod protection links whitelist view
Emote Protection Usage
Action Command
ON !mod protection emotes on
OFF !mod protection emotes off
MAX !mod protection emotes max <number>
Symbol Protection Usage
Action Command
ON !mod protection symbols on
OFF !mod protection symbols off
MAX !mod protection symbols max <number>
Capital Letter Protection
Action Command
ON !mod protection caps on
OFF !mod protection caps off
MIN !mod protection caps max <number>
MAX !mod protection caps max <number>
Long Message Protection Usage
Action Command
ON !mod protection long on
OFF !mod protection long off
MAX !mod protection long max <number>
Slow Mode
Action Command
SET !slowmode <number>
STOP !slowmode stop
